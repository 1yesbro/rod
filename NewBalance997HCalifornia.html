History

In 1991, New Balance introduced the 997 model to the world, which brought the 99X range to a whole new level of technology and aesthetics, and over the years has taken pride of place among truly classic models in the brand's collection. Today we are pleased to present you a fresh look at the legend - 997H! Drawing inspiration from the archival models and the historical heritage of the brand, we have developed a model that is ready to meet all the requirements of the modern rhythm of big cities and has every chance of becoming a new classic of everyday style.


The top of the model, while maintaining the authentic detail of the design, combines natural suede and leather and is complemented by mesh elements. This combination of materials allows you to provide the most comfortable fit in the foot, improve airflow and opens up a huge space for experiments with color and textures. The midsole of the model combines the compression layer of C-CAP® foam and ENCAP® technology, which, combined with a rigid insert in the heel zone, provides a completely new level of shock absorption and stabilization of the foot. Focusing on unsurpassed comfort, especially relevant in the modern rhythm of life, we took care not only about the softness, but also the lightness of the shoe, and complemented the model with the Ground Contact EVA outsole, which has excellent grip, flexibility and lightness.
